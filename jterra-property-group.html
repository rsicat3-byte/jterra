Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

New to building in Melbourne? Here are the questions we get asked most often.

What is the First Home Owner Grant (FHOG) in Victoria?
The Victorian FHOG provides eligible first home buyers $10,000 when buying or building a new home valued up to $750,000. JTerra guides you through the application process as part of our service.
How much deposit do I need to build a new home?
In most cases you need a minimum 5–10% deposit. With the FHOG and other government incentives, some clients build with as little as 5% deposit. Our finance brokers will assess your individual situation.
How long does it take to build a new home in Melbourne?
Typically 12–18 months from contract signing to handover, depending on the builder and complexity. We provide ongoing updates throughout construction so you're never left in the dark.
Do I need to find my own land separately?
No — we offer house and land packages where both are bundled together. We also help clients who already own land find the right home design to match their block's dimensions and orientation.
What costs are involved beyond the build price?
Additional costs typically include stamp duty (on the land), council fees, utility connections, landscaping, fencing, window furnishings, and moving costs. We provide a full breakdown upfront so there are no surprises.
Can I customise the home design?
Absolutely. Our builder partners offer extensive customisation through their studio selection process — from kitchen finishes and flooring to façade choices and room configurations. We help you navigate all options.
